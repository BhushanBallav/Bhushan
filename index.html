About Me

I am a PhD researcher in the Department of Electronics and Electrical Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati. My research focuses on spoof surface plasmon polariton (SSPP) based microwave filters, metamaterial-inspired RF structures, and flexible microwave devices for next-generation wireless communication systems.

Research Interests

  • Spoof Surface Plasmon Polaritons (SSPP)
  • Microwave Filters
  • Metamaterials and Metasurfaces
  • Flexible RF and Microwave Devices
  • Electromagnetic Wave Propagation
